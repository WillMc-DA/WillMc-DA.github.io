History of Eurovision scores - Visualised

Collaborative help to create Tableau visuals before the 2023 competition.

The Eurovision:

A song competition held between nearly 40 countries - most are in Europe, but includes Russia, Austrailia and Israel. It is currently run with 2 semi-finals, where for 2023 10 countries will emerge from each Semi-final. And a Final that includes those 20, and 6 set countries - The "Big Five" countries France, Germany, Italy, Spain, the United Kingdom and the winner from the previous year (Ukraine).

What are we doing?:

Creating Tableau Visuals to showcase our Tableau skills.
- You make the visual, we give input/help!

Why?:

  • Working together (bouncing off each other) we can make better visuals to showcase on our Portfolios/LinkedIn - and up our Tableau skills.
  • The Eurovision will be topical in and around 9th-13th May - This, I would think, means you will get more people looking at your visuals/portfolio.

RULES:

There are only 2!
  • DO NOT claim somebody elses work as your own! - basically, give credit... for the orig CSV file, any visuals that you have 80% recreated, etc.
  • If you do analytical work on the CSV file, share it with everybody on the Slack Channel
    - no point in us all doing the same work on the numbers. (Put your name in the file name)
Regarding the first 'rule' - You can say who 'Inspired' you in your visual. As I did for my A6 project.

So, what should I visualise?:

Whatever you want! - I know, easy to say... but here are some suggestions:
  • The total of the Eurovision scores since day one!
  • Trends of the Semi-finals - since 2008
  • Trends of the Telephone voting - since 2016
  • Trends of your own Country - maybe even report in dual-language!
  • The 'bloc' voting thing - Nordic/former Soviet neighbouring countries vote for each other
  • Nobody remembers 2nd place - well, maybe you do!
  • And to go REALLY advanced (trying to visualise) - countries voting for neighbours (not the 'bloc' thing)
