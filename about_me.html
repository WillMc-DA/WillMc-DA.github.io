A skilled Data Cleaner, Inconsistency Spotter and Data Interrogator.

Me:

I am passionate about helping people - will discuss volunteering below - and can be found answering fellow CareerFoundry students questions at all hours.
I want to help my employer too! If I see that 'somebody' needs to ... book in that product ... move those pallets ... clean that area ... I step in and just do it.

Me and Data:
